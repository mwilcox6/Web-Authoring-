Homestyle Potatoes and Sausage

by Morgan Wilcox

Growing up, I would always make breakfast for my younger siblings.They were all so picky and they never wanted anything I prepared. That changed the day I made my famous homestyle potatoes and sausage.
I usually eat this meal as a quick breakdfast option. I am currently a pescatarian so I don't eat the sausage but I still prepare it for my siblings. Today, I will walk you through my infamous recipie.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sized potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes
  • 1 large green pepper
  • 2 tps of red pepper flakes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tps of salt
  • 2 tsp of pepper
  • 13oz Turkey Sausage
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable oil

What to do ?

  1. First, rinse all of your vegetables
  2. Peel potatoes and cut into small cubes
  3. Place into a strainer and strain now cut potatoes
  4. Season with 1 tps of salt
  5. Place frying pan on stove with 3/4 cups of oil over medium heat
  6. While the oil heats, slice the turkey sausage into small circles
  7. Place potatoes into heated oil
  8. Dice green pepper, garlic, onion, and tomatoes into small cubes
  9. Continuously mix potatoes,making sure that each side is cooked
  10. Fry for about 5-10 minutes or until golden brown
  11. Reduce heat, remove potatoes from pan and drain oil
  12. Leaving only a little oil remaning in the pan and place back on stove over medium heat adding add the garlic and 1 tsp of salt
  13. Satuee for about a minute and add sausage
  14. Cook sausage for about 7 minutes almost fully cooked
  15. Add onion, tomatoes, and bell pepper to the pan
  16. Mix continuously
  17. Cook until vegetables are tender and sausage if fully cooked
  18. Season with red pepper flakes and pepper
  19. Place over low heat to let the season cook in for about 3 minutes
  20. Reduce heat, Plate and Serve
